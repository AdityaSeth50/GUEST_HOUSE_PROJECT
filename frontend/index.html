About Us

Our Story

IIEST Guest House provides comfortable accommodation for visiting faculty, parents, alumni, and other guests of the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur.

Our guest house features well-appointed rooms, a restaurant serving delicious meals, and all the amenities needed for a pleasant stay. Located within the beautiful campus, we offer a peaceful retreat with easy access to the institute.
